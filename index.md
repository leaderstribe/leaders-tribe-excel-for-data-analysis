# Excel for Data Analysis for Women

**A practical training program empowering women with data skills — by Leaders Tribe.**

---

Welcome to the official resource hub for **Excel for Data Analysis for Women** — a structured, community-centred training program designed to help women build real confidence and competence in Microsoft Excel for data analysis.

Whether you are a facilitator delivering this program, a participant working through the curriculum, or a partner exploring our approach, you will find everything you need here.

---

## What This Program Offers

- **Practical, hands-on learning** — no theory overload, just real skills
- **Two progressive levels** — beginner and intermediate, each with a capstone project
- **Train-the-Trainer model** — every graduate becomes a multiplier of knowledge
- **Nigerian-context datasets** — practice files that feel real and relevant
- **Facilitator-ready materials** — session guides, workbooks, assessments, and more

## Who This Is For

This program welcomes women aged 18 and above from any background — students, entrepreneurs, NGO staff, early-career professionals, and community advocates. No prior Excel experience is needed for Level 1.

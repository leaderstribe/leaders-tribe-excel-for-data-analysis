# TTT Completion Checklist

**Leaders Tribe — Train-the-Trainer Program**

Use this checklist to make sure you have completed every step of the Train-the-Trainer requirement before submitting your reflection report.

---

## Before Your Sessions

- [ ] I have identified at least 10 people I will teach
- [ ] I have confirmed that at least 50% of my learners will be women
- [ ] I have planned my sessions using the TTT Session Plan Template
- [ ] I have downloaded or printed the materials I need from the `level-1/` folder
- [ ] I have set up my attendance log
- [ ] I have shared session details with my learners

---

## During Your Sessions

- [ ] I am recording attendance for every session
- [ ] I am covering all core Level 1 topics (navigation, data entry, formulas, IF, charts)
- [ ] I am including at least one hands-on practice activity per session
- [ ] I am taking at least one photo per session (encouraged but not required)

---

## After Your Sessions

- [ ] I have reached at least **10 learners** in total
- [ ] At least **50% of my learners were women**
- [ ] I have completed the TTT Reflection Report
- [ ] I have attached or noted photos from at least one session
- [ ] I have submitted my Reflection Report to my Leaders Tribe facilitator at leaderstribe22@gmail.com

---

## After Submission

- [ ] My facilitator has confirmed receipt of my report
- [ ] My TTT requirement has been marked as complete
- [ ] I have received my Level 1 Certificate (or confirmed it is being processed)
- [ ] I have been enrolled in Level 2 (if applying)

---

*Congratulations on becoming a trainer and multiplying your impact!*

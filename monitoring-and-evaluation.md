# Monitoring and Evaluation

---

## Overview

Leaders Tribe is committed to tracking program impact and using evidence to improve the *Excel for Data Analysis for Women* program over time. This page outlines the key metrics, data sources, and reporting processes used to monitor and evaluate the program.

---

## Monitoring Framework

| Metric | Indicator | Data Source | Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Training Attendance** | % of participants completing sessions | Attendance logs | Weekly |
| **Learning Progress** | Improvement between pre- and post-assessment scores | Test results | Each Level |
| **Skill Application** | % of participants applying Excel skills in real-world tasks | Follow-up surveys | 3 months post-training |
| **Community Engagement** | Number of women leading peer learning or TTT activities | Participant reports | After Level 2 |
| **TTT Reach** | Total new learners reached through the TTT model | TTT reflection reports | After each cohort |
| **Gender Inclusion** | % of TTT learners who are women | TTT reflection reports | After each cohort |

---

## Data Collection Tools

### Attendance Tracking
- **Tool:** Session attendance log (template in `templates/attendance-log-template.csv`)
- **Completed by:** Facilitator after each session
- **Stored in:** `facilitator-resources/`

### Assessments
- **Pre-assessment:** Administered at the start of Level 1 and Level 2 to capture baseline knowledge
- **Post-assessment (capstone):** Evaluated using the capstone rubric at the end of each level
- **Tools:** Available in `level-1/assessments/` and `level-2/assessments/`

### Participant Follow-Up Survey
- Administered approximately 3 months after program completion
- Captures real-world application of skills learned
- Includes both quantitative ratings and short open-ended reflections

### TTT Reflection Reports
- Submitted by participants after completing their Train-the-Trainer delivery
- Captures reach, gender inclusion, session quality, and participant experience
- Template available in `train-the-trainer/ttt-reflection-report-template.md`

---

## Qualitative Feedback

In addition to quantitative metrics, regular reflection sessions capture:

- Participant confidence levels (before and after training)
- Perceived value of the program
- Challenges experienced during learning
- Leadership growth observed during the TTT experience

Facilitators are encouraged to take brief notes after each session on group dynamics, engagement levels, and any notable moments.

---

## Expected Outcomes by Stage

### Short-Term (During and Immediately After Training)
- 40 women trained per year in beginner and intermediate Excel data analysis
- Participants demonstrate improved confidence and competence using digital tools

### Medium-Term (3–6 Months Post-Training)
- Participants apply skills to work, education, or business operations
- Women-led peer learning groups established in communities

### Long-Term (12+ Months)
- Growing network of women using data to inform decisions and lead initiatives
- TTT graduates serving as co-facilitators in future Leaders Tribe cohorts
- Participants recognized as Certified Women Data Ambassadors

---

## Reporting

After each cohort, the Leaders Tribe team compiles a short **Cohort Impact Report** summarizing:

- Number of participants enrolled and completing each level
- Average pre vs. post assessment improvement
- Total TTT reach (new learners reached through the multiplier model)
- Participant feedback highlights
- Lessons learned and program adjustments for the next cohort

These reports support accountability to partners and funders, and inform ongoing program improvement.
